Built for advisors who've outgrown

Cary Street Partners is one of the fastest-growing independent wealth management firms in the Southeast. We recruit experienced advisors and advisor teams who are ready to build something meaningful — without the constraints of a wirehouse or the isolation of going fully independent.

Everything you need to build
your best practice.

Your Book. Your Clients. Your Practice.

Why Advisors Choose Us

Independent by structure.
Exceptional by standard.

No Proprietary Products

You'll never be required to sell a firm-manufactured product. Our open architecture means you access the full investment landscape — guided entirely by client needs.

Fiduciary, Always

Every advisor at Cary Street Partners operates under a fiduciary standard. It's not a marketing claim — it's the legal framework that governs every recommendation we make.

Competitive Economics

Transparent, advisor-friendly payout structure with no hidden fees or platform charges that erode your earnings. We believe the economics should reward the people who do the work.

Planning-First Technology

21 Offices, National Reach

How It Works

A thoughtful transition.
No disruption to your clients.

01
Confidential Conversation

A private, no-obligation call with our advisor development team. We learn about your practice, your clients, and what you're looking for. Nothing is shared without your consent.

02
Practice Assessment

We analyze your book together — AUM composition, revenue, client demographics — and model what your practice would look like on our platform, including compensation projections.

03
Transition Planning

Our dedicated transition team handles the operational complexity. Client communications, account transfers, technology onboarding — all coordinated to minimize disruption and protect your relationships.

04
Day One Readiness

From your first day, you're fully supported — compliance, technology, investment resources, and a team around you. The goal is that your clients notice no gap and experience only an improvement.
